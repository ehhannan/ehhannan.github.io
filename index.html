About me

PhD student in Applied Mathematics at Polytechnique Montréal with a strong academic background in mathematics, computer science and physics. My research topics are optimization, mathematical modeling and machine learning. More precisely, I am interested in developing derivative-free optimization methods for deep learning problems, such as hyperparameter tuning.
